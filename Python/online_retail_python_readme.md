# Online Retail Data Analysis

## Dataset Source
[Online Retail Dataset from Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/lakshmi25npathi/online-retail-dataset?resource=download)

## Overview
This project performs comprehensive analysis on an online retail dataset, focusing on product performance, customer behavior, and return patterns. All detailed code, analysis, and visualizations are available in the Jupyter Notebook file `online_retail_python_analysis.ipynb`.

## Analysis Categories

### 1. Product Analysis
- Identifying Best-Selling Products
  - Monthly Sales Trend for Top 3 Best-Selling Products
- Top Revenue-Generating Products
- Relationship Between Quantity Sold and Total Revenue per Product
- Top Premium Products by Average Unit Price
- Identifying Underperforming Products: Low Demand & Low Revenue
- Identifying Consistently High-Demand Products based on Demand Stability
- Product Revenue Concentration (Pareto 80/20 Analysis)

### 2. Analysis of Returned Products
- Top Returned Products
- Products with High Return Rates
- Return Rate vs. Total Transactions: Identifying High-Risk Products

### 3. Customer Behavior Analysis
- Customer Purchase Frequency Analysis
  - Customer Purchase Frequency Distribution with Top 5% Threshold
  - Top 10 High Purchase Frequency Customers
- High Return Rate Customers
- Top Customers by Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
- Relationship Between Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) and Return Behavior
- RFM Analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)
- Customer Segmentation Using RFM Analysis
- RFM Segment-Based Revenue Contribution

### 4. Time Series Revenue Trend
- Analyzing revenue over time to identify seasonal patterns and business growth

## Requirements


1. Download the dataset from the Kaggle link above
2. Open `online_retail_python_analysis.ipynb`
